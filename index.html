Server Rules

These rules ensure a fair and clean Vanilla+ experience for everyone.

1. No cheating or scripting

Any form of hacks, scripts, macros or third‑party advantages results in a permanent ban.

2. No exploiting

Abusing bugs, terrain glitches or unintended building spots is not allowed.

3. No harassment or hate speech

Trash talk is fine — targeted harassment, slurs, doxxing or threats are not.

4. Reports & suspicious activity

If you see anything suspicious or need to report a player, you must do it through our Discord.

5. Respect the Vanilla+ experience

No teaming beyond the vanilla team limit. No cross‑team alliances that break balance.

6. Raiding rules

24/7 raiding is allowed. No raid griefing unless you intend to take over the base.

7. No advertising

Do not promote other servers, Discords or communities.

8. Common sense

If you think “this is probably not allowed,” it probably isn’t.
